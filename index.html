Description

Racket Ran 2025 is coming — get ready for the biggest Table Tennis showdown of HBTU!

What is Racket Ran?

Racket Ran ek inter–club Table Tennis tournament hai jisme HBTU ke players ek doosre ke against compete karte hain. Teams mixed hoti hain taaki sabko equal chance mile – seniors, juniors, boys aur girls sab ek hi platform par khelte hain.

Format & Teams

  • • Total 5 teams participate karengi.
  • • Har team mein 3 boys aur 2 girls honge.
  • • Har match multiple events ka combination hoga (singles + doubles).
  • • Har event ka result team ke overall points mein add hoga.

Match Events

  • • Boys Singles – 1 ladka.
  • • Girls Singles – 1 ladki.
  • • Boys Doubles – 2 boys ka pair.
  • • Girls Doubles – 2 girls ka pair.
  • • Mixed Doubles – 1 boy + 1 girl.

Basic Table Tennis Rules

  • • Match generally best of 5 games hoga (pehle 3 games jeetne wala winner).
  • • Ek game 11 points ka hota hai, aur jeetne ke liye 2 point ka lead zaroori hai.
  • • Har player 2–2 points ke liye serve karta hai, phir service change.
  • • Serve hamesha table par pehle apni side, phir opponent ki side bounce karega.
  • • Doubles mein serve diagonally (right se right) diya jata hai.
  • • Rally tab tak chalta hai jab tak ball net mein, bahar, ya ek bounce se zyada na ho jaye.
  • • Racket ke alawa body se table ko zor se move karna / haath rakhna allowed nahi.
